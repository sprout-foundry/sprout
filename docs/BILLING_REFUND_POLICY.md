# Billing Refund Policy

## Overview

This document outlines when refunds are available, how to request them, and what to expect during the process. We aim to handle refund requests fairly and promptly.

---

## Refund Eligibility Criteria

We issue refunds in the following situations:

### 1. Accidental Charges

If you were charged by mistake — such as an unintentional plan upgrade, an unexpected renewal you didn't intend to keep, or a duplicate transaction — you are eligible for a refund.

### 2. Service Outages

If a significant platform outage prevented you from accessing paid features for a material portion of your billing period, you are eligible for a prorated refund or credit covering the affected time.

### 3. Duplicate Charges

If your account was charged more than once for the same billing period due to a system error, the duplicate charge will be fully refunded.

### 4. Billing Errors

If we made an error in your billing calculation — such as charging the wrong plan rate or applying an incorrect proration amount — we will correct the charge and refund the difference.

### What Does Not Qualify for a Refund

- Voluntary cancellations mid-cycle (you retain access until the end of your paid period).
- Dissatisfaction with feature set or platform performance without a corresponding outage.
- Charges older than one billing cycle (contact support to discuss options).

---

## Refund Process Steps

To request a refund, follow these steps:

### Step 1: Contact Support

Reach out to our support team via:
- **Email**: support@platform.com
- **In-app**: Use the help center or contact form in your account settings

### Step 2: Provide Required Details

Include the following information in your request:
- Your account email address
- The billing period or invoice in question
- The reason for the refund request
- Any supporting details (e.g., outage dates, screenshots of duplicate charges)

### Step 3: Review and Approval

Our support team will review your request within **1–2 business days**. We may contact you for additional information if needed. Once approved, the refund will be processed to your original payment method.

### Step 4: Confirmation

You will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been initiated. This email includes the refund amount, the expected timeline, and a reference number for your records.

## Refund Timeline

| Refund Type | Processing Time | Notes |
|-------------|----------------|-------|
| **Partial refund** | 3–5 business days | Prorated credits or partial charge reversals (e.g., service outage credits, billing corrections). |
| **Full refund** | 5–10 business days | Complete charge reversals (e.g., accidental charges, duplicate charges). |

Timelines begin once the refund is approved and initiated. Actual processing time may vary depending on your bank or card issuer. If you do not see the refund within the expected window, check with your financial institution or contact our support team.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I get a refund if I cancel my subscription?

No. When you cancel, you retain access to your paid plan until the end of your current billing period. No partial refunds are issued for cancellations.

### How long does a refund take?

Partial refunds are processed within 3–5 business days. Full refunds are processed within 5–10 business days. Additional time may be needed depending on your bank.

### Can I request a refund to a different payment method?

No. Refunds are always issued to the original payment method used for the charge, as required by payment processor policies.

### What if my refund request is denied?

If your request does not meet our eligibility criteria, our support team will explain the reason in our response email. You may escalate the matter by replying to the response or contacting us again for further review.

### Can I get a refund for a service outage?

Yes. If a significant outage prevented you from accessing your paid features during your billing period, you are eligible for a prorated credit or refund covering the affected time.

### Will a refund affect my subscription status?

A refund does not automatically cancel your subscription. Your paid plan remains active through the end of your current billing period unless you choose to cancel.

### Can I request a refund more than once?

Refund eligibility is evaluated on a per-request basis. Repeated refund requests may be reviewed on a case-by-case basis by our support team.

### How do I track the status of my refund?

You will receive a confirmation email with a reference number once your refund is initiated. You can use this reference number to check status by contacting support.
