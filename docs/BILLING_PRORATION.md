# Billing Proration Policy

## Overview

When you change your subscription plan mid-cycle (upgrade or downgrade), we automatically calculate prorated credits and charges so you only pay for what you use. This ensures fair billing regardless of when you make changes to your account.

---

## How Proration Works

When you upgrade or downgrade your plan before your current billing cycle ends, the following applies:

### Upgrading Your Plan

1. **Credit for unused time**: The remaining days of your current plan are calculated as a credit based on your current plan's daily rate.
2. **Charge for new plan**: You are charged for the remaining days at your new plan's rate.
3. **Net adjustment**: The credit is applied against the new charge, and you are billed only the difference.

### Downgrading Your Plan

1. **Credit for unused time**: The remaining days of your current plan are calculated as a credit based on your current plan's daily rate.
2. **Charge for new plan**: You are charged for the remaining days at your new plan's rate.
3. **Net credit**: If the credit exceeds the new charge, the balance is applied as a credit to your account for future invoices.

### The Formula

For any plan change mid-cycle, we calculate:

```
Prorated Credit = (Current Plan Monthly Price ÷ Days in Billing Cycle) × Unused Days Remaining
Prorated Charge = (New Plan Monthly Price ÷ Days in Billing Cycle) × Days Remaining in Cycle
Net Adjustment  = Prorated Charge − Prorated Credit
```

- If the **Net Adjustment** is positive, you are charged the difference immediately.
- If the **Net Adjustment** is negative, the difference is applied as a credit to your account.

### When Proration Takes Effect

- **Upgrades**: Take effect immediately. You gain access to new features right away.
- **Downgrades**: Take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. You retain access to your current plan's features until then.

## Examples of Proration Calculations

### Example 1: Upgrading from Free to Pro (Mid-Cycle)

**Scenario**: You are on the Free plan ($0/mo) and upgrade to Pro ($29/mo) on day 10 of a 30-day billing cycle.

| Item | Calculation | Amount |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Prorated credit (Free plan) | ($0 ÷ 30) × 20 remaining days | $0.00 |
| Prorated charge (Pro plan) | ($29 ÷ 30) × 20 remaining days | $19.33 |
| **Net adjustment** | $19.33 − $0.00 | **$19.33 charged** |

You are charged $19.33 immediately and your Pro plan starts right away.

---

### Example 2: Downgrading from Pro to Free (Mid-Cycle)

**Scenario**: You are on the Pro plan ($29/mo) and downgrade to the Free plan ($0/mo) on day 20 of a 30-day billing cycle.

| Item | Calculation | Amount |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Prorated credit (Pro plan) | ($29 ÷ 30) × 10 remaining days | $9.67 credit |
| Prorated charge (Free plan) | ($0 ÷ 30) × 10 remaining days | $0.00 |
| **Net adjustment** | $0.00 − $9.67 | **$9.67 credited to account** |

Your downgrade takes effect at the next billing cycle. The $9.67 credit is applied to your next invoice.

---

### Example 3: Upgrading from Pro to Team (Mid-Cycle)

**Scenario**: You are on the Pro plan ($29/mo) and upgrade to the Team plan ($79/mo) on day 15 of a 30-day billing cycle.

| Item | Calculation | Amount |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Prorated credit (Pro plan) | ($29 ÷ 30) × 15 remaining days | $14.50 credit |
| Prorated charge (Team plan) | ($79 ÷ 30) × 15 remaining days | $39.50 |
| **Net adjustment** | $39.50 − $14.50 | **$25.00 charged** |

You are charged $25.00 immediately and your Team plan starts right away.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I upgrade my plan at any time?

Yes. You can upgrade your plan at any point during your billing cycle. The upgrade takes effect immediately, and you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your current cycle.

### When does a downgrade take effect?

Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. You retain access to your current plan's features for the remainder of the current cycle.

### Will I get a refund if I downgrade?

No full refunds are issued for downgrades. Instead, a prorated credit is applied to your account and will be used against your next invoice.

### Can I see the proration breakdown on my invoice?

Yes. Every proration charge or credit is itemized on your invoice with a clear breakdown of the credit, charge, and net adjustment. You can view your invoices in your account's billing settings.

### What happens to my data when I downgrade?

Your data and projects are preserved. If your new plan has lower usage limits, you may lose access to features that exceed those limits, but no data is deleted.

### Is there a fee for changing plans?

No. Changing plans — whether upgrading or downgrading — does not incur any additional fees beyond the prorated difference.

### What if I switch plans multiple times in one billing cycle?

Each change is calculated independently. The proration is always based on your current plan's rate versus the new plan's rate for the remaining days in the cycle. You may see multiple line items on your invoice, but the total will always reflect the correct amount for the time used.

### How do I check my current billing cycle dates?

You can view your current billing period, next billing date, and plan details in your account's billing settings page.
